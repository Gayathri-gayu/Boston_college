Boston College @ Glance

Education with a heart and soul—and the power to transform

Boston College of Engineering and Technology was instituted in the year 1985. The college is accredited with “A” Grade by National Assessment and Accreditation Council, Bangalore. The college has been granted autonomous status by UGC during 2011 and approved by AICTE, New Delhi and has a permanent affiliation to Anna University, Chennai. Housed in an area of 130 acres and with total built-up area of 80,000 sq.m., the college has excellent infrastructure-academic buildings, laboratories, library, hostels and play grounds where the students experience pleasant and congenial intellectual environment to grow with unique and value based academic experience.

About Us

Boston college was instituted in the year 1985 by the V.L.B. Trust, a charitable organization founded by the great philanthropist and industrialist, late Sri.V.L.BalaKrishna Naidu. Late Sri.S.Vankatram, over two and half decades of tireless effort and guidance brought the college to its present preeminent position. The college is approved by AICTE,New Delhi and the UG programmes are accredited by NBA.

Our Mission

To produce intellectual and technological giants, who endowed with unfathomable depths of inner strengths and unbounded technical skills, shall deliver the bounties of their knowledge for technological gains of the society and for the spiritual and emotional uplift of the mankind without any reservation whatsoever.
